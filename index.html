Hello! I'm Paranjal, a UX Designer (who dabbles in far too many other things from graphic design & branding to CAD & jewellery design) currently based in Perth, Western Australia. I bring a blend of creativity and analytical thinking to every project. My goal is to create intuitive interfaces that resonate with users, driven by empathy and a commitment to solving complex problems.
